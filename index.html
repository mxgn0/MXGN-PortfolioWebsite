About

I am Max, 3D Artist born 1995 in Saxony, Germany. My Focus contains procedural physical simulations, motion design and contemporary art. I started modeling and animating with Autodesk 3Ds Max and Maya at the age of 12. I continued creating my first visuals like Covers, Intros or Advertising in Cinema4D together with the use of After Effects, Photoshop and Illustrator. After working for a small marketing agency and a few private client tasks in Saxony while studying Media-Informatics and interactive Entertainment i decided in 2018 to focus on more detailed designs, which were only able with Plugins in C4D and third-party-software. Learning the powerful tool houdini in 2018 sensitized me for procedural content creation.

Projects

  • bachelor thesis at Hochschule Mittweida (B.Sc.) - representation of physical sheerwaves
  • procedural scaffolding experiment - industrial study
  • human portraits - creating procedural combination workflows, facetracking, expression
